Why Us?

With Over 20+ years experience as a pastry chef it's no secret, owner Sonny Kirik has a passion for his bakery.
Specialising in French style cakes and famous for his Almond,Chocolate Croissants and Lemon tarts.
A Treat of France breads and pastries are all made fresh on the premises.
Accompanied by great coffee from Piazza D'Oro and their friendly staff, A Treat of France is a must!
2013 will see A Treat of France open it's second cafe in the heart of Melbourne; Southbank. A Treat of France was also judged the best patisserie in town in The Age Good Food Guide 2012.
